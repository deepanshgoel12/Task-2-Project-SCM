Veg. Manchurian Recipe

  1. Prepare the Manchurian Balls
  2. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, fry the Manchurian balls until they turn golden brown.
  3. Prepare the Manchurian Sauce
  4. Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, and chili sauce to the pan. Stir well to combine
  5. Pour in the water and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  6. Add the cornstarch slurry while stirring continuously to thicken the sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Once the sauce reaches the desired consistency, add the fried Manchurian balls to the pan. Gently coat the balls with the sauce.
  8. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot

Hakka Noodles

  1. Cook the Hakka noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add sliced onions and stir-fry for a couple of minutes until they start to soften.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for about 4-5 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  5. Add the cooked noodles, soy sauce, and chili sauce to the pan. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Toss everything together and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.
  8. Serve your quick and tasty Hakka noodles hot

Non-Veg. Biryani

  1. Cook the rice until 70% done, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a pot, sauté spices and onions until golden brown
  3. Add chicken, tomatoes, and spices. Cook until chicken changes color and tomatoes soften.
  4. Stir in yogurt and cook until the mixture thickens
  5. Layer partially cooked rice with chicken mixture, mint, and coriander leaves.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes
  7. Fluff the biryani and serve hot with your choice of sides.

Tandoori Chicken

  1. Make deep slits in the chicken.
  2. Mix yogurt, tandoori spice, lemon juice, oil, ginger-garlic paste, paprika, and salt to create a marinade.
  3. Coat the chicken with the marinade and refrigerate for 2 hours or overnight.
  4. Grill or bake at 450-500°F (230-260°C) for 15-20 minutes until fully cooked and charred.
  5. Garnish with lemon and coriander.
  6. Serve with naan, rice, or your preferred sides.

Chilli Chicken

  1. Marinate chicken with egg, cornstarch, soy sauce, salt, and black pepper. Rest for 20-30 minutes.
  2. Stir-fry marinated chicken until golden and cooked. Set aside.
  3. Sauté garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Add onions and bell peppers, stir-fry until they soften.
  4. Return chicken to the pan, mix with vegetables.
  5. Pour a sauce mixture of soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, sugar, and salt over chicken. Stir to coat.
  6. Add water, simmer until sauce thickens and coats the chicken.
  7. Garnish with chopped spring onions.
  8. Serve with rice or as an appetizer. Enjoy your chili chicken!
